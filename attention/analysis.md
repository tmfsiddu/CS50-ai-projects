# Analysis

## Layer 5, Head 2

This attention head appears to focus on adjectives attending to the nouns they modify.

Example Sentences:
- The red car was fast.
- A small dog barked loudly.

## Layer 7, Head 9

This attention head appears to link pronouns to the nouns they refer to.

Example Sentences:
- John lost his wallet because he was careless.
- Mary said she would return later.
